About
Newington FC

Newington FC are a North Belfast based football club that play in the NI Football League's Intermediate Premier League. Our Seconds play in the Balymena & Provincial Junior Division One and our U20 Development Squad play their games in the NIFL Championship Development League North. Newington Youth FC's U18, U17 and U16 squads play in the South Belfast Youth League.
