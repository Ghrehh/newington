Newington FC

Newington FC are a North Belfast based football club that play in the NI Football League's Intermediate Premier League. Our Seconds play in the Balymena & Provincial Junior Division One and our U20 Development Squad play their games in the NIFL Championship Development League North. Newington Youth FC's U18, U17 and U16 squads play in the South Belfast Youth League.

Archive

Starting life as Jubilee Olympic in 1979, Newington FC rose quickly through the amateur ranks, making histroy and winning countless trophies along the way. We need your help to preserve and celebrate our history! We're planning to build a new archive section as part of our new website. If you've any old photographs, newpaper clippings or documents relating to our journey from a pub team in 1979 to an Irish League club today, we'd love to hear from you! you can scan and email files directly to website@newingtonfc.com or if you'd like to arrange for us to make a copy of your original files we can do that and return them to you. Help us preserve our very special history; get in touch today.

Digital Transformation

Newington FC are working in partnership with a local design studio to create a fit-for-purpose modern online presence in 2017. We know that fan engagement extends well beyond 90 minutes, so together with Burns Bros. & Co. we will be building a brand new website, further developing our social media platforms and building internal apps to support our day-to-day operations. Stay tuned for updates on our digital strategy!
